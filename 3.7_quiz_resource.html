This quiz is on derivatives, chain rule, product rule, quotients, and acceleration. The first question goes over acceleration.
Positive slope = Positive acceleration. Negative slope is a negative acceleration. No slope means the car is at a constant speed.
Anytime the line crosses over the x axis it is switching directions.

The 4th question uses an "Approximate Equation". This means you will do math>8 in your calculator, then enter the numbers to find
the slope. To put it in slope intercept, do y-y1 = m(x-x1), where y1 is the y value (Which can be found by solving the initial equation
with the x value given), x1 is the x value given, and m is the slope.
