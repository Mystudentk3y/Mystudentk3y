Parallel Lines Help

This notes sheet shows the two diffrent type of lines you'll see in Geometry 10A.

- Parallel lines are "two lines that will never touch" meaning if you have two parallel lines, no matter how long they are they will never touch.

- A transversal line is a "line that cuts through two or more lines".
