Mystudentk3y

Mystudentk3y is a homework help resource where highschool students can contribute to help make homework, test and study resources more accessible to those who need them.
We hope to create a database fillid with every worksheet you will encounter in highschool, and are constantly striving to improve our website.

Use the navigation bar at the top to jump between subjects.

You can submit work, ask questions, or submit worksheets you want us to do by emailing mystudentk3y@gmail.com.

When submitting work, please provide:

- Subject
- Grade
- Name of Class
- Name of assignment
- Pictures or links with answers and questions
- Grade if possible, as well as those you got wrong. If you haven't recieved a grade or can't see which ones you got wrong, that is fine

When Submitting worksheets you want us to do, please provide:
- Subject
- Grade
- Name of Class
- Name of assignment
- Pictures or links with questions
- Certain assignments may require payment to do. Worksheets are usually 5$, depending on the workload. Anything 7th grade and under is free


We apologize for any work that may be hard to see.

We are aware Mystudentk3y doesnt work on most school networks, in order for Mystudentk3y to work either turn off your wifi or use a vpn. A good option is Nordvpn but it does cost money.

About Us

Mystudentk3y was created to help students who may have missed class time and need to catch up or students that may feel lost in class. Mystudentk3y is not supposed to be used as a cheating source and we do not condone cheating. You can also upload your own work to Mystudentk3y if you would like.
